About AirWV

Empower WV — community eco monitoring is a community air-quality initiative for West Virginia, powered by the open-source AirWV project. It collects readings from community PurpleAir sensors, stores long-term history, and surfaces trends, anomalies, and alerts. It grew out of the Kanawha Valley Air Quality Monitoring project (Create WV, WVCAG, and partners).

Data shown is from community sensors and is provided as-is for awareness, not regulatory use. Source & docs: github.com/createwv/airwv.
