🛢️ Abandoned wells near our VOC sensors

Abandoned & orphaned gas wells can vent natural gas and hydrogen sulfide (H2S). Our community sensors' VOC reading is a relative gas-response index (it responds to hydrocarbons and H2S, but it is not an H2S monitor). This lines up each sensor's VOC with the abandoned wells around it — context and a place to look, not proof any well caused a reading. Sorted by nearby wells.
