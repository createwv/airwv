Sources: USGS NWIS real-time gauges (flow, gage height, and some pH / oxygen / conductivity / turbidity) and the Water Quality Portal (EPA/USGS lab & grab samples — E. coli, metals like iron/aluminum/manganese, sulfate, nitrate, TDS — across hundreds of sites).
